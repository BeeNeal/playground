Cranberry Sauce

Ingredients

  • 12 oz fresh cranberries
  • 3 oranges
  • 1 granny smith apple
  • 4-5 dates

    • Instructions

    1. Rinse cranberries: Place the cranberries in a colander and rinse them. Pick out and discard any damaged or bruised cranberries.
    2. Squeeze enough orange juice for 1 cup - resserve some orange zest
    3. juice the granny smith (optional ingredient)
    4. Pit dates and blend with the juice
    5. Combine all ingredients and bring to a boil
    6. Lower the heat, bring to a simmer for 10 minutes or until 75% of the cranberries have burst
    7. Add in orange zest
    8. Remove from heat and let cool to room temp, then transfer to fridge

    Optional

    Suggestions:

  • pecans
  • Sub half orange juice with pomegranate juice
