Christmas Salad

Ingredients

Salad

  • Mixed Greens
  • Pomegranate Seeds
  • Toasted Pecans
  • d'Anjou Pear or Bosc Pear
  • Shaved Fennel

    • Dressing

  • 1/4 cup Balsamic vinegar
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • medium shallot, minced
  • 1 pinch each sea salt and black pepper

    • Instructions

    1. Whisk together all dressing ingredients
    2. Toss the greens with the dressing
    3. Toss half the ingrdients in with the greens
    4. Place rest of ingredients as garnish
    5. Enjoy!
