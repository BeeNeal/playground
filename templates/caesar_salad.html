Caesar Salad

Ingredients

  • Dijon mustard
  • Worcestershire Sauce
  • Lemon Juice
  • Anchovy paste
  • Olive Oil
  • salt/pepper
  • crushed garlic
  • Romaine

    • Instructions

    1. Grind pepper directly on the leaves
    2. Combine all Ingredients and mix well
    3. toss the romaine with the dressing
    4. enjoy!

    Optional

    Add additional items to the romaine. Suggestions:

  • artichoke hearts
  • hearts of palm
  • hemp seeds
